# Getting Started at Acme Corp

Welcome to Acme Corp! This guide will help you get up and running during your first week.

## First Day

1. Pick up your laptop from IT (Building A, Room 101).
2. Complete the onboarding portal at `onboarding.acme.internal`.
3. Set up your development environment using the engineering handbook.
4. Schedule a 1:1 with your manager.

## Accounts You'll Need

| Service | URL | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Email | mail.acme.internal | Provisioned automatically |
| Slack | acme.slack.com | Join #general and your team channel |
| GitHub | github.com/acme-corp | Ask your manager for team access |
| Jira | jira.acme.internal | Used for task tracking |
| 1Password | team.acme.1password.com | Company password manager |

## First Week Goals

- Complete compliance training (see Data Privacy policy).
- Read the Engineering Handbook and API Style Guide.
- Set up your local development stack.
- Submit your first pull request (even a small one!).

## Getting Help

- **IT issues**: it@acme.internal or slack #it-support
- **Engineering questions**: slack #engineering
- **HR questions**: hr@acme.internal

Welcome aboard!
