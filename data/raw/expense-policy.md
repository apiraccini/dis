# Expense Policy

Travel and expense reimbursement rules at Acme Corp.

## Eligible Expenses

| Category | Limit | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Flights (economy) | No limit | Book at least 14 days ahead for best rates |
| Hotels | $250/night | Use preferred partners for direct billing |
| Meals | $75/day | Alcohol not reimbursable |
| Ride share / taxi | $50/trip | Receipt required |
| Internet / phone | Actual cost | For remote work days, up to $50/month |

## Approval Levels

| Amount | Approval Required |
|---|---|
| $0 – $500 | Manager approval |
| $500 – $2,000 | Director approval |
| $2,000+ | VP approval |

## How to Submit

1. Collect receipts (digital photos accepted).
2. Submit via the expense portal at expenses.acme.internal within 30 days.
3. Attach the approval if required (system auto-routes for manager approval).
4. Reimbursement arrives within 5 business days via payroll.

## Non-Reimbursable Items

- First-class or business-class flights (unless medical necessity, pre-approved).
- Personal entertainment (streaming, gym, sightseeing).
- Fines or penalties (parking tickets, late fees).
- Gifts for non-employees over $25.

## Remote Work Stipend

Full-time remote employees receive a $500/year home office stipend. Submit with a single receipt or multiple receipts totaling at least $500. This does not require manager approval.
